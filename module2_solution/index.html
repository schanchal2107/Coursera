Olympic Gold Medalist

Abhinav Apjit Bindra

Honorary Lieutenant Colonel Abhinav Apjit Bindra is an Indian businessman and retired sport shooter. He is the first and one of only 2 Indians to win an Individual Olympic Gold Medal. With more than 150 medals in his 22-year career, he is the recipient of the Padma Bhushan from the Government of India.

Neeraj Chopra

Neeraj Chopra VSM is an Indian track and field athlete who competes in the javelin throw. As of August 2021, he is ranked second internationally by World Athletics. A Junior Commissioned Officer in the Indian Army, Chopra is the first track and field athlete to win a gold medal for India at the Olympics.

Men's Hockey(1928)

India stunned the world on its hockey debut in the Olympics. The Men in Blue, who were a colony of the British Empire back then, won their first-ever gold medal in the Amsterdam Olympics, 1928. This marked the beginning of something special. However, India went on to win 8 Gold medals in Hockey.
